## ğŸš» Why Restroom Data Matters

Most major mapping platforms only partially cover public restrooms. Common issues include:

- Missing or incorrect locations  
- No details about accessibility or baby-change facilities  
- No indication of opening hours or seasonal closures  
- Poor coverage in **regional**, **remote**, and **parkland** areas  

DunnyDash exists to explore how a **focused, amenity-first dataset** can improve:

- Travel planning  
- Tourism experience  
- Disability and accessibility support  
- Long-distance driving and freight work  
- Outdoor recreation (camping, hiking, fishing, etc.)
